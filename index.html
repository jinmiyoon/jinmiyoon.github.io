I am a JINA-CEE postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Physics at the Univerisity of Notre Dame and Joint Institute of Nuclear Astrophysics- Center for the Evolution of the Elements (JINA-CEE). I study ancient, most metal-poor stars still living today to understand the first-star nucleosynthesis, the very early star-forming environment, and Galactic chemodynamical evolution in the early Universe..

In 2021, I will move to the Space Telescope Science Institute (STScI) as a Senior Staff Astronomical Data Scientist at the Mikulski Archive for Space Telescopes (MAST).
